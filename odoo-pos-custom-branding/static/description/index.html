Odoo POS Custom Branding

Remove generic Odoo branding from your Point of Sale and replace it with your own. This module is for Odoo 18 Community and lets you set a custom logo and hide Odoo branding on the POS header, saver screen, customer display, and receipts.

Features

  • Custom POS logo – Upload a logo per POS (header, saver screen, customer display).
  • Hide Odoo branding – Option to remove “Powered by Odoo” and Odoo logos.
  • POS header/navbar – Your logo in the center of the POS top bar.
  • Saver (sleep) screen – Your logo when the POS is idle.
  • Customer display – Your logo and no Odoo text on the customer-facing screen.
  • Receipts – “Powered by Odoo” removed from receipt footer.

Configuration

Go to Point of Sale → Configuration → Point of Sale, open a POS config, and use the Branding & Logos section:

  • Enable Hide Odoo Branding to remove Odoo logos and text.
  • Upload POS Brand Logo to use your logo on header, saver screen, and customer display.
